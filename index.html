Berlin, Arm aber sexy

Berlin Umsonst is an app which list events happening in and around Berlin. Most of these events are free, but most comedy-events expect a small donation ca.5-10$. So if you have a good time make sure show them your appreciation. Events listed were gathered using web-srapping technologies, so NO GUARANTY here of safety and quality, If you decide to attend, you'll be doing at your own risk.

Try the App

There are more than 500 upcoming events in more than 8 different categories listed, lot of stuff you should be looking forward to!

Comedy

Enjoy the upcoming stars of stages. Many unknown comedians made their debut in these small pubs and alot are still at it. The comedy scene is fill with very and somewhat less talented artist just waiting for their moment to shine.

Sports

Always wanted to try a new sport but too lazy for it. Now your chance, try one of the free sport events without comiting yourself, if you dont like it, you dont have to go back.

Arts

The berlin art scene is somewhat unique. Difficult to say what is really art here, Painting, grafiti, street performance, Berlin is bursting out with energy, sometimes a little too much energy.
